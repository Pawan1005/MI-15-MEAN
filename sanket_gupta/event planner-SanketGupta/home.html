Destination Wedding
Destination wedding venues you should bookmark for 2022!
A beautiful backdrop is a canvas against which you experience one of the most cherished moments of your life, the right wedding venue can do wonders for your celebrations. While the pandemic may have reduced the frequency of destination weddings in the last year, the pace has fortunately picked up with couples favoring local destinations that are just as beautiful as international ones! With that in mind, we’ve curated a list of wedding hotspots that are bound to be on the top of many wedding wishlists in the coming year.

1. Goa

Goa is one destination that gives you the best of both worlds. It’s serene and glamorous at the same time! A firm favorite amongst Indian couples, it’s dotted with pristine beaches, old churches, picturesque forts, luxurious as well as budget resorts, and it exudes a laid-back vibe that’ll make your wedding seem like a vacay. This well-connected destination has everything for a sunkissed wedding by the waves.

2. Mussoorie

If you’re someone who’s always yearned for the hills, then this is the best place to tie the knot! Mussoorie is encircled by lush green hillocks as far as the eyes can see, sheltered by a pollution-free cerulean blue sky and located close to the capital city of Delhi, this hill station checks all the boxes for that surreal dream wedding.

3. Udaipur

Have you been dreaming of a royal wedding? Then look no further than Udaipur. An epitome of luxury, Udaipur is a destination that is a go-to for couples who have envisioned a regal wedding for themselves. With a multitude of palatial lakeside hotels, forts and heritage palaces to choose from, you are sure to be spoilt for choice at the ‘City of Lakes’. Besides, it’s extremely well connected to Mumbai, Delhi, Gujarat and other major metro cities by plane, bus, or train!
